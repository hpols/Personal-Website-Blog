MusGym

Musical Games & Exercises

Music should be fun, both for the musicians and for the listeners. That’s why I want to offer small games and exercises to make music more accessible to you in a playful way. You do not always have to take the instrument to hand. Do not only play music … play with the music.

Whether you want to work on a specific aspect or just want to try something new, this site will offer you suggestions and possibilities to discover your inner musician with joy.
